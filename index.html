About Me

My name is Chris Kennedy, but my friends know me better as Young Kennedy or "YK." I use this nickname as my music producer alias and stage name.

Music is may passion and is a major part of my life aside from school and socializing with friends. You can catch me DJing at house parties and events around the valley where I like to play electronic dance music and hip hop. You can follow my original productions at this link

Education

I attended high school at Arizona School for the Arts (ASA) where I gained a college preparatory education and was trained to play various forms of classical music. While at ASA, I learned piano, voice, guitar, and percussion. Going to ASA had a profound impact on my current passion for music.

I was later accepted into Barrett, the Honors College at ASU. I am still attending Barrett with a major of Digital Culture: Media Processing. With this major, I am learning digital design and programming with the intention of creating and manipulating media.
